THE MASTER BULIDER

BROVIK

[Lowering his voice a little.] I don't want the poor children to know how I am.

SOLNESS

Yes, you have been looking very poorly of late.

BROVIK

It will soon be all over with me. My strength is ebbing--from day to day.

SOLNESS

Won't you sit down?

BROVIK

Thanks--may I?
